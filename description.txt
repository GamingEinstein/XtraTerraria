[c/0000ff:Xtra Terraria] is a mod which aims to improve various areas of the game. This includes:
= Early Pre-Hardmode Summoner and Mage
= Various Biome and Mini-Biome improvements
= Reduce redundant and unnecessary grind
= Reduce the rush of Endgame
= And more

This mod is a passion project that I plan on working on for a while, so if something is wrong or doesn't seem right, feel free to ask and suggest!
I am also looking for people to help contribute! Join the Discord if you are interested or just want to hang around!

Thanks for reading!
- [c/ff00ff:GamingEinstein]

Currently features:
- 4 Items
- 2 Placeables
- 5 Weapons
- 1 NPC
- 1 Biome
- Plus more to come...
