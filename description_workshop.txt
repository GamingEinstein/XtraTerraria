Xtra Terraria is a mod which aims to improve various areas of the game. This includes:
[list]
[*] Early Pre-Hardmode Summoner and Mage
[*] Various Biome and Mini-Biome improvements
[*] Reduce redundant and unnecessary grind
[*] Reduce the rush of Endgame
[*] And more
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
This mod is a passion project that I plan on working on for a while, so if something is wrong or doesn't seem right, feel free to ask and suggest!
I am also looking for people to join the team! Join the Discord if you are interested or just want to hang around!

Thanks for reading!
